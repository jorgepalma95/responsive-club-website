Lisbon Swimmers Club

Meet up. Swim. Celebrate life.

Welcome to our Swimmers Club website! We are a community of passionate swimmers of all levels, from beginners to competitive athletes. Our club offers a variety of programs for swimmers of all ages and abilities, including learn-to-swim classes, stroke technique clinics, and competitive swim teams.
In addition to our regular programs, we host fun social events, such as pool parties and team outings, to foster a sense of camaraderie and friendship among our members. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where swimmers of all backgrounds and abilities can thrive and have fun.
Whether you're looking to swim for fitness, recreation, or competition, our Swimmers Club has something for everyone. Join us today and take the plunge into a world of fun and fitness!

-Bryce R. - financial advisor/frequent swimmer since 2018
